Product Description

Autocallable products (‘autocalls’) are structured products linked to an underlying asset, which can automatically mature (or ‘kick out’) prior to their scheduled maturity date if certain pre-determined market conditions have been met with regard to the underlying asset. An autocall is an investment package which comprises several financial instruments; a zero coupon bond combined with call and put options referencing the underlying asset.
